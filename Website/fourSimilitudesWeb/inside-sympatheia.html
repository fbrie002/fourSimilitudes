4. Sympatheia


"Sympathy transforms, alters, but in the direction of identity, so that if its power were not counterbalanced it would reduce the world to a point, to a homogeneous mass, to the featureless form of the Same.

This is why it is compensated for by its twin, antipathy, which maintains the isolation of things and prevents their assimilation".

This computational representation of the play of sympathies is produced by a physics engine where forces of attraction and repulsion are simulated to create this mesmerising dance, a visualisation for the constant counterbalancing between these two opposite poles of resemblance.

When pressing anywhere on the screen, the tentacles multiply and the movement grows in intensity and intricacy.

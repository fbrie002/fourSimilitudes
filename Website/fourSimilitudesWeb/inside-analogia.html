3. Analogia


"His pulse beats in his veins as the stars circle the sky according to their own fixed paths".

This computational representation of the inivisble ties of Analogy displays two orbs circling one another, moved by the rhythm and sound of a heartbeat and a lonely synth reverberating into the black void of space.

Press anywhere on the screen to play the soundscape and watch the visuals react: the orbs light up and dim down, like lovely starts following each other's circular motion.
